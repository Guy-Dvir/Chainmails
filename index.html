About

Email as you know it, but much better

Email as we know today is based on a protocol called SMTP, Simple mail transfer protocol. As it names states, this is a simplified protocol to exchange hypertext and attatchments.

Chainmails mission is to enable email achieve its full potential as a platform for distributed structured communication. We do that by integrating a distributed ledger on top of the existing SMTP network, creating a multi layered adaptable communication network:

  1. SMTP - Global adopted network to exchange unstructured, un-encrypted documents packets.
  2. Chainmails - A distributed network to faciliate the exhcange of RSA encryption keys & signatures,and a global ledger for stamping email transactions and enabling smart-contracts.
  3. deApps - Distributed email applications for specific business domain, communication of structured data, with the potential to improve any workflow.

Security

Chainmails introduces the first truly distributed end-to-end email encryption. no matter what is your platform of choice, you can secure your email communications with anyone on any email platform with no hassle.

The Problem:

  • Encryption and signatures are not native practices of the email protocol.
  • Anyone who get your mail can read the private information in it.
  • No built-in method in SMTP protocol for exchanging encryption keys`
  • Users’ data is owned by providers and is used for monetization
  • Email encryption solutions are platform dependent and platform specific
  • Email is susceptible to hacking (password stealing, MitM, phishing, BEC)

Existing solutions:

Today’s offering in the space of email security are ranging from Encryption services, spam protection, email fraud protection and …
What is common to all services is that they are all centralized solution for the security problem, meaning - they all share the same Achilles' heel

  • Single point of failure
  • One company controls and holds your public/private keys
  • Unique and specific to a specific organization / email provider / mail client

What does ChainMails offer:
  • Security
  • Works for every email server
  • No need to migrate to a new Email service
  • Easy implementation over current client
  • A decentralized solution
